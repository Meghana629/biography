Biography

Indira Gandhi,born on November 19,1917,in Allahbad,India,was the daughter of Jawaharlal Nehru,the first Prime Minister of independent India.Educated in India and at the University of oxford,she became an influential political figure,first 1966 to 1977 and again from 1980 until her assassination in 1984.Her tenure included significant policies like the nationalization of banks in 1969 and the "Green Revolution" to boost agricultural productivity.She also led India during the Bangladesh Liberation War in 1971.Her leadership was marked by controvarsy,notably the Emergency Period from 1975 to 1977,during which she imposed a state of emergency that curtailed civil liberties.
